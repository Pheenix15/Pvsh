Questions we think you may have
What is group economics?
Group economics refers to a collective of individuals pooling their resources to achieve a common goal faster than would be possible if pursued individually, as examplified by aspiring to a wealth of information, knowledge, motivation, and networking that would otherwise take several additional years to acquire alone.
